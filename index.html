World War 2

Prior to the Second World War, the Kingdom of Thailand, had always been a largely neutral country. The increasingly belligerent Japanese Empire would come to change this.

Upon joining with the Japanese Empire, Thailand began to order weapons, tanks, and equipment from them. Most notably, a small number of Japan’s staple tank.
